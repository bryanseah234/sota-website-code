Release of 2015 International Baccalaureate Examination Results

Published on Jan 28, 2016

Release of 2015 International Baccalaureate Examination Results


​

5 January was an exciting day for our 2015 graduating classes as they came together for the release of the International Baccalaureate Results.

SOTA is proud to achieve 100% pass rate in both the International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme (IBDP) and the IB Career-related Programme (IBCP) Examinations!

This year, we are also pleased to have SOTA’s first batch of IBCP students graduating, with most of them having secured places in established local and international art institutions and conservatories.

All the best to our 2015 graduates as they embark on their new journey!
