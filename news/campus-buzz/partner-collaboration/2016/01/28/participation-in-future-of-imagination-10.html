Partner Collaboration

Future of Imagination 10

Published on Jan 28, 2016

Future of Imagination 10


SOTA students Grace Ling and Aqilah Hassan were invited by Future of Imagination 10 (FOI 10) Artistic Director Mr Jason Lim, who is also a SOTA faculty member, to take part as artists showcasing their drawings during the week long international event held in November 2015. FOI 10 is an international performance art event and for 7 evenings, the students took turns to create drawings of the live performances.
