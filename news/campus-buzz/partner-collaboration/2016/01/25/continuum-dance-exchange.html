Continuum Dance Exchange

Published on Jan 25, 2016

Continuum Dance Exchange

 
During the Continuum Dance Exchange, part of the M1 Contact Contemporary Dance Festival organised by The Human Expression (T.H.E) Dance Company, five SOTA IBCP Dance students joined other dance students from LASALLE College of the Arts, Nanyang Academy of Fine Arts, Korean National University of the Arts, Victoria College of the Arts, and New Zealand School of Dance for a spirited dance exchange performance. The exchange also included a pre-show talk with Singapore’s leading dance educators and overseas guest schools sharing about their programmes, training and experience.
