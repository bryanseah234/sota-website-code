Stellar Stage Fundraising Concert

Published on Jan 22, 2016

Stellar Stage Fundraising Concert

Held on 29 October, the inaugural SOTA Stellar Stage Fundraising Concert celebrated the creativity of our students while raising funds for students in need of financial assistance and for student development programmes.

The concert featured 6 student performances, consisting of 4 singing, 1 dancing (by the group Dil Se Naach) and 1 beatboxing performance (by Izad). Besides the students, there were also 2 guest performances: our very own teachers, Ms Yang Tien and Ms Cheryl Lim from the Music Faculty who put up a music performance while one of our parents, Mr Shafyre, performed with his band.

Stellar Stage received encouraging support from the staff, fellow students and distinguished guests. We would like to thank those who took time to attend the concert, donated money, performed or helped out in various ways to make Stellar Stage a success!
