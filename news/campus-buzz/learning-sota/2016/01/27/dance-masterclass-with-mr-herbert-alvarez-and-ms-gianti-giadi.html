Learning @ SOTA

Dance Masterclass with Mr Herbert Alvarez and Ms Gianti Giadi

Published on Jan 27, 2016
Dance Masterclass with Mr Herbert Alvarez and Ms Gianti Giadi


​

During the post-exams period, our dance faculty invited two Asean dance artists to share with the Year 3 students their dance cultures, choreographic inspirations and styles, as well as their personal dance stories.

Mr Herbert Alvarez and Ms Gianti Giadi graciously took time off their schedule to share with the dancers some interesting things on their dance form. On the final day of the sharing, the dancers put up a performance based on their interactions and learning from Mr Herbert Alvarez and Ms Gianti Giadi.
