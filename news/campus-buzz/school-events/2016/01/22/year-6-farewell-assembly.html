Year 6 Farewell Assembly

Published on Jan 22, 2016

Year 6 Farewell Assembly


The Year 6 Farewell Assembly took place on 9 October to symbolically mark the end of our Year 6s' time as students in SOTA and to appreciate the efforts of our staff and parents who have accompanied our graduating cohort along this journey. It was truly a memorable and emotional event for all our Year 6s!

All the best to all our Year 6s as they prepare for the upcoming IB examinations and move on to the next lap of their journey!
